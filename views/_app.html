Philadelphia Police Advisory Commission Complaints

2009 – 2012

This map plots 2009 – 2012 complaints filed with the Philadelphia Police Advisory Commission by citizens against Philadelphia Police officers.

The data is published by Open Data Philly. According to Open Data Philly, the data comes directly from the Police Advisory Commission Complaint Database.

The data does not include additional complaints filed with Philadelphia Police Internal Affairs.
